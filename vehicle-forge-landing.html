The Class System

Know What You Can Hurt

The DiceForge Class system rates both vehicles and weapons on the same A–Z scale. A vehicle's Class comes from its total Toughness. A weapon's Class comes from its average damage plus AP. Compare the letters and you know instantly whether a fight is fair, desperate, or hopeless.

Classes A through G map directly to the Science Fiction Companion's Classes I through VII. The parenthetical roman numeral in every stat block is provided for GMs using the SFC Heavy Metal rules. No conversion needed.

"When your players ask 'can our guns hurt that thing?' you don't need to do the maths. If they're packing B-class weapons against a C-class target, the answer is 'barely.' If they're facing a D, the answer is 'run.' The Class system is a conversation shortcut disguised as a stat." — Vehicle Forge Companion Guide
